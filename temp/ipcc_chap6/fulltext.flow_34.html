6.4.2.3
Hydroelectric Power 14
Hydropower is technically mature, proved worldwide as a primary source of renewable electricity, and 15
may be used to balance electricity supply by providing flexibility and storage. The LCOE of 16
hydropower is lower than the cheapest new fossil fuel-fired option. However, the future mitigation 17
potential of hydropower depends on minimizing environmental and social impacts during the planning 18
stages, reducing the risks of dam failures, and modernising the aging hydropower fleet to increase 19
generation capacity and flexibility (high confidence). 20
