electrification for different regions will depend on the economics and availability of alternative 15

6.6.2.4
Alternative Fuels in Sectors not Amenable to Electrification 18
Net-zero energy systems will need to rely on alternative fuels – notably hydrogen or biofuels – in several 19
sectors that are not amenable to electricity and otherwise hard to decarbonize (medium confidence). 20
Useful carbon-based fuels (e.g., methane, petroleum, methanol), hydrogen, ammonia, or alcohols can 21
