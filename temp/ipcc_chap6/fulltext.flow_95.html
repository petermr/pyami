CDR is not intended as a replacement for emissions reduction, but rather as a complementary effort to 31
offset residual emissions from sectors that are not decarbonized and from other low-carbon technologies 32
such as fossil CCS (McLaren et al. 2019; Gaffney et al. 2020; Iyer et al. 2021). 33
